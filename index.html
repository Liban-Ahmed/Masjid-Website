Tawba Islamic Center

The Tawba Islamic Center of Des Moines, a nonprofit organization and 501(c)(3) mosque, was established in 2014. Its mission is to cater to the needs of the greater Des Moines community, serving as a hub for Islamic knowledge, spirituality, social gatherings, and communal support.

Come visit us!

If you're approaching Tawba Islamic Center in Des Moines from Interstate 235, take the exit onto 21st Street. As you travel along 21st Street, you'll find our center located at 1411 21st St, Des Moines, IA 50311. Keep an eye out for our distinct features.For those arriving from the north on 21st Street, you'll spot our center on the left side. If you're traveling from the south on 21st Street, continue until you reach the intersection with Interstate 235. Tawba Islamic Center will be on your right. Parking is conveniently available at Ibrahim Grocery & Fashion and Citizens For Community Improvement, ensuring a hassle-free visit. We look forward to welcoming you soon!
